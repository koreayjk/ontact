Teacher Setup Guide

How to Create & Send Your Zoom Keys

Connect your own Zoom account to ONTACT so your class meetings are created automatically. It only takes about 5–10 minutes. 🎥

⬇ Download PDF version

Why are we asking for this?

To run your classes smoothly, ONTACT creates each Zoom meeting for you, automatically — using your own Zoom account. To allow this safely, Zoom gives you 3 keys. You create them once, send them to us, and you never have to make a meeting link by hand again.

The 3 keys you will send us

You will find all three on the same screen inside Zoom.

Before you begin

  • You need your own Zoom account and must be able to sign in to it.
  • You should be the Owner / Admin of that account (your normal personal account is fine — you own it).
  • Recommended: a Licensed / Pro plan, so classes aren't cut off by the 40-minute limit of free accounts.
  • Use a computer (not the phone app) — this is done on the Zoom website.

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Open the Zoom App Marketplace & sign in

    Go to marketplace.zoom.us and click Sign In (top-right) with your Zoom email & password.

  2. Start building a new app

    Top-right, click Develop → Build App.

  3. Choose “Server-to-Server OAuth”

    Find the card named Server-to-Server OAuth and click Create.

    Important: Pick Server-to-Server OAuth — not “OAuth”, “SDK”, or “Webhook”. Other types won't work.

  4. Give your app a name

    Type a simple name like ONTACT Class and click Create.

  5. Copy your 3 keys 🔑

    You're now on the App Credentials page — all 3 keys are here:

    On the Zoom screenWhat to do
    Account IDClick Copy.
    Client IDClick Copy.
    Client SecretClick the eye icon to reveal, then Copy. Keep it private.
    Tip: You can return to this page anytime to copy the keys again.

  6. Fill in the basic information

    Click Continue, then enter Company Name (you can use ONTACT), your Name and Email. Click Continue.

  7. Add the required permissions (Scopes)

    On Scopes, click Add Scopes and add:

    • Search meeting → “View and manage all user meetings” (meeting:write:admin)
    • Search user → “View users” (user:read:admin)

    If you only see longer “granular” names, just add the ones about creating/managing meetings and viewing users. Then Continue.

  8. Activate your app

    Go to Activation → Activate your app. You should see a green “Activated” ✅.

  9. Send the 3 keys to ONTACT

    Copy your Account ID, Client ID, and Client Secret and send them back using the format below. We'll connect them for you.

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to pay anything?

No. Creating this app is completely free. (A paid Zoom plan is only recommended so classes aren't limited to 40 minutes.)

Is this safe? Can ONTACT see my Zoom password?

Yes, it's safe. You're not giving us your password. These keys only let our system create class meetings on your account, and you can turn it off anytime by deleting the app.

I can't find “Develop” or “Build App”.

Make sure you're signed in on a computer browser (not the phone app). Still stuck? Send us a screenshot and we'll help.

I missed copying the Client Secret. Is it gone?

No — go to Develop → Manage, open your app, and the App Credentials page shows all three keys again.

Will this change my own personal Zoom meetings?

No. Your normal Zoom account, meetings, and settings stay exactly the same.
